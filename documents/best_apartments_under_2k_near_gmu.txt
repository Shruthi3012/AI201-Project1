Title: Best Apartments Within 25 Min from GMU Under $2K

Original Question:
What are the best apartment options within 25 minutes of George Mason University (GMU) for under $2,000 per month, preferably in Arlington, Alexandria, Fairfax, or Falls Church?

Community Suggestions and Experiences:

Commenter 1:
Recommended looking for studios in Clarendon, Rosslyn, and Courthouse areas.

Suggested renting from private landlords rather than large apartment complexes.

Reported seeing several studios and some one-bedroom apartments within the target budget.

Commenter 2:
Recommended using Apartments.com and local Facebook housing groups to search for available apartments.

Commenter 3:
Questioned whether apartments under $2,000 are realistically available in Arlington, Alexandria, Fairfax, or Falls Church due to the area's high housing costs.

Commenter 4:
Suggested considering Woodbridge in Prince William County.

Estimated that it can be approximately 25 minutes from GMU without heavy traffic.

Mentioned that affordability may require sacrificing amenities such as in-unit washer and dryer.

Commenter 5:
Suggested Trillium Apartments in Fairfax because many GMU students have lived there.

Mentioned that it is located near the Mosaic District.

Commenter 6:
Strongly advised caution regarding Trillium Apartments (formerly Circle Towers).

Reported that the complex has a long-standing negative reputation and recommended extensive research before renting there.

Commenter 7:
Reported concerns about Trillium Apartments including:
- Frequent break-ins
- Non-residents sleeping in the building
- Pest problems
- Poor maintenance conditions

Commenter 8:
Suggested that finding a roommate would significantly increase affordable housing options in the desired locations.

Additional Notes:

The original poster later indicated a willingness to increase the budget to approximately $2,100.

Many commenters agreed that finding a desirable one-bedroom apartment under $2,000 in Arlington, Alexandria, Falls Church, or Fairfax is difficult without:
- Having roommates
- Renting from private landlords
- Accepting older buildings or fewer amenities

Overall Summary:

Housing under $2,000 near GMU in Arlington, Alexandria, Fairfax, and Falls Church can be difficult to find. Community members recommended searching through private landlords, Apartments.com, and Facebook housing groups. Clarendon, Rosslyn, and Courthouse were suggested as potential locations. While Trillium Apartments were mentioned because of their lower cost and proximity to GMU, multiple commenters strongly warned about safety, maintenance, and pest issues. Most respondents felt that increasing the budget or sharing housing with roommates would provide substantially better options.
