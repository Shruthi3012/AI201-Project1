Title: Incoming GMU Student Looking for Affordable Housing in Virginia Square / Ballston

Original Question:
An incoming GMU student was looking for affordable housing near Virginia Square or Ballston with Metro access. The student hoped to spend between $1,000 and $1,400 per month and preferred a roommate arrangement where each person had a private bathroom.

Community Responses:

Commenter 1:
Noted that Arlington is expensive and asked whether other locations had been considered.

Suggested:
- Springfield
- Falls Church
- Annandale
- Parts of Alexandria

Commenter 2:
Recommended living somewhere between Arlington and Fairfax to balance affordability and commuting.

Specifically identified Falls Church as a strong option.

Commenter 3:
Explained that GMU Fairfax Campus does not have a direct Metro station.

Students commonly:
- Take Metro to Vienna Station
- Use the CUE Bus or GMU Shuttle to reach campus

Commenter 4:
Provided transportation details.

Reported that:
- CUE buses are free
- GMU shuttles operate regularly between Vienna Metro and campus
- Shuttle departures typically occur every 30 minutes

Commenter 5:
Warned that commuting from Arlington to Fairfax using Metro and buses can be very time-consuming.

Estimated that a one-way trip may exceed two hours depending on transfers and wait times.

Commenter 6:
Shared a personal example.

Reported paying approximately:
- $1,400 per month for half of a two-bedroom, two-bathroom apartment
- Utilities not included

Commenter 7:
Noted that Northern Virginia has a very high cost of living.

Estimated:
- Median one-bedroom apartment rent around $2,200 per month

Suggested:
- Renting with roommates
- Sharing a two-bedroom or three-bedroom apartment

Commenter 8:
Explained that a shuttle operates between Mason Square (Arlington Campus) and Fairfax Campus.

Mentioned that living near Mason Square is convenient but expensive.

Commenter 9:
Recommended looking for housing near:
- Virginia Square Metro
- Vienna Metro
- East Falls Church
- West Falls Church
- Clarendon

Reasoning:
These locations provide access to Metro service and GMU transportation options.

Overall Summary:

Most commenters agreed that Arlington and Ballston are expensive areas where a $1,000-$1,400 housing budget generally requires roommates. Falls Church, Springfield, Annandale, and Alexandria were repeatedly recommended as more affordable alternatives. Transportation to GMU Fairfax Campus usually involves a combination of Metro, shuttle, and bus services. Living near Metro stations can improve access to both Fairfax and Arlington campuses but may increase rent costs.
