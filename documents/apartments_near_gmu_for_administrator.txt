Title: Apartments Near George Mason University BUT for Administrator (Not Student)

Original Question:
A prospective GMU employee relocating from New York City was seeking apartment recommendations near George Mason University. Desired features included:

- Budget up to $2,200
- In-unit washer and dryer
- On-site gym

Community Responses:

Commenter 1:
Believed the stated budget might be difficult depending on the apartment type and location.

Commenter 2:
Recommended Robinson Apartments.

Reasons mentioned:
- Located very close to GMU
- Positive reviews
- Within the stated budget

Commenter 3:
Mentioned that GMU employees have access to the university gym at a low cost.

Suggested that paying extra for an apartment gym may not be necessary.

Commenter 4:
Noted that many apartment options exist within approximately five miles of campus that could fit the budget and requirements.

Commenter 5:
Recommended considering areas near Metro stations.

Suggested locations:
- Vienna/Fairfax Metro area
- Dunn Loring area

Benefits mentioned:
- Easier access to Washington, DC
- Better access to regional airports
- Reduced traffic concerns

Commenter 6:
Reminded prospective renters that traveling from Vienna Metro to GMU Fairfax Campus still requires a shuttle ride.

Overall Summary:

Several commenters believed that apartments meeting the stated requirements can be found within a few miles of GMU. Robinson Apartments received a direct recommendation. Areas near Vienna Metro and Dunn Loring were suggested because they provide convenient access to Metro transportation and the broader Northern Virginia and Washington, DC region. Community members also highlighted the value of GMU's employee gym access, which may reduce the importance of finding an apartment with its own fitness center.
